How to Play Fuse Field

Drag orbs to empty cells to create matches!

When three or more identical orbs are adjacent, they fuse into a higher level orb.

Level 6 orbs fuse into crystals, which grant bonus points.

Try to create the longest chain of fusions!

The game ends when the board is completely filled with orbs and crystals.
